Lauren's Quarantine Cookbook

here.

For my final project in this Journalism Design and Toolkit course, I wanted to create my own Quarantine Cookbook (for non-cooks). This idea came from a place of wanting to help other students and provide super easy recipes with minimal ingredients. As a non-cook myself, I wanted to take away the extra stress of figuring out different recipes. Especially during these trying times, the last thing many of us want to do is cook complex meals or have to order takeout.

This Quarantine Cookbook uses minimal ingredients and steps. Enjoy!
